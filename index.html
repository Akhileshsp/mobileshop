About Us

MV Mobile Service Center is a trusted service center in Bangalore, specialized in repairs of motherboards, mobile phones, Iphones. MV Mobile Service Center provides the best repair services to customers looking to get the device repaired. Our great passion has rewarded us for successfully rectifying over 2000+ device repairs during the span of 4-5 years of making the customers satisfied beyond their needs and expectations.

MV Mobile Service Center provides services in the most easiest and convenient way of getting your gadgets repaired across all local hubs of Bangalore. We have a very well equipped staff of repairing gadgets majorly for International brands such as Samsung , Redme , Mi , Nokia , LG , HTC ,Motorola , Asus , Xiaomi , Huawei , Sony , BlackBerry , MicroMax , Lenovo , Vivo , OPPO , Lava and all series of Apple iPhones such as iPhone 4 & 4s, iphone 5 & 5s and iPhone 6 & 6s ,iPhone 7, iPhone 6s, and iPhone X. Our skilled team of technicians is experienced in multi-brand devices and specialized exclusively in repairing MAC brand and products for optimal performance.
